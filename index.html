Our Story

Where Beauty Meets Serenity

Sanvila was born from a singular vision: to create a sanctuary in the heart of Kolkata where every person—regardless of gender—could experience world-class grooming and soul-refreshing spa treatments under one roof.

Our expert team of stylists and therapists bring years of training and passion to every session. From precision haircuts to rejuvenating spa rituals, we tailor each experience to your unique needs.
